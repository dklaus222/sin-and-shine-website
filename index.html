ABOUT SIN & SHINE

Sin & Shine was founded in 2026 by Deidre Klaus (Sweet Dee) in Tampa, Florida — built on precision, intention, and a refusal to deliver anything average.

What started as an obsession with transformation turned into a luxury mobile detailing experience designed for clients who expect more. Not faster. Not cheaper. Just better.

The name Sin & Shine represents duality — grit and polish, edge and refinement. We handle the work most people overlook and turn it into something flawless.

We specialize in high-end auto detailing, paint enhancement, and curated finishes. Every service is done with precision, consistency, and attention to detail that speaks for itself.

This isn’t volume-based detailing. We don’t rush appointments, and we don’t cut corners. We take on fewer clients so every vehicle gets the level of care it actually deserves.

As a women-owned brand, Sin & Shine exists to raise the standard — in quality, in service, and in how this industry operates.

— Deidre Klaus, Founder & CEO

FLAWS & SIN ART CONTEST

At Sin & Shine, we don’t celebrate perfection — we celebrate what’s raw, overlooked, and real.

A few times a year, when the calendar aligns with Friday the 13th, we open submissions for our Flaws & Sin Art Contest — a curated drop featuring artists who understand the balance between edge and refinement.

This contest was built around the day Sin & Shine was created — January 13th — and carries that same energy: intentional, a little rebellious, and impossible to ignore.

Artists are invited to submit original artwork inspired by the Sin & Shine brand — not just a logo, but the full identity. Duality, luxury, imperfection, femininity, edge, and transformation — whatever sin & shine means through your lens.

We’re not looking for copies.
We’re looking for interpretation.

Grand Prize:
The selected artist will have their design turned into a limited-edition release, featured across our brand, and professionally produced.

You’ll receive:
– Full credit across Sin & Shine platforms
– Compensation for your artwork
– A featured drop tied to a Friday the 13th release

This isn’t mass production.
This is a statement piece.

Created on the 13th. Released on the 13th.

If your work stands out, we’ll make sure it’s seen.

Submission Guidelines & Rules

To qualify for the Flaws & Sin Art Contest, all submissions must follow the guidelines below:

– Artwork must be original and created by the submitting artist
– Designs must be inspired by the Sin & Shine brand identity (not copied logos)
– Submissions should reflect themes such as duality, luxury, imperfection, femininity, edge, or transformation
– Artwork must be suitable for apparel or print production
– No copyrighted or trademarked material may be used

How to Qualify

– Submit a portfolio link and original artwork concept
– Include a short explanation of your design and its meaning
– Be available for communication if selected

Submission Timeline

Submissions open 30 days prior to each Friday the 13th.
All entries must be submitted no later than 7 days before the release date.

The selected artist will be announced on Friday the 13th, alongside the official release.

Submissions open on every Friday the 13th throughout the year.
